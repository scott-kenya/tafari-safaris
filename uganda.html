City Tours

Take a tour around Kampala.Kampala has the best night life experience in East Africa. The land is filled with historical sites ranging from the Kasubi tombs to the famous Martyrs shrine in Namugongo. It's rich in food and a vibrant culture

Adrenaline Junkies

Conquer thine Fears. Jinja is a town where all fearless people go, It is filled with lots of attractions from bungee jumping, water rafting and floating restaurants. This is for the fearless at heart

Visit Wakanda . Visit the famous Wakanda region and the great Queen Elizabeth National park. Don't forget the equator that defies the laws of gravity and the hot springs in the western hills
