City Tours

Take a tour around Nairobi.Nairobi and it's environs has a lot to offer from; historical sites, zip lining, game drives, shopping and excavations.

Beach Relaxation

Relax at the beach. Mombasa has the best all year round weather for relaxing and a great number of water sports like fishing, deep-sea diving, swimming with the dolphins, skiing, speed boats, just to mention but a few.

Safaris and Camping

Dine in the wild and enjoy nature. For the lovers of nature, wild animals and outdoor camping not forgetting the amazing tented camps, This is the place for you. Climb mountains, view amazing sites and eat bush meat to your fullest.
